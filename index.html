Learn about personalized medicine on the blockchain

OmicCoin provides a secure and decentralized database of personalized genomic data on the blockchain.




Personalized medicine on the Blockchain has the potential to be one of the most dramatic healthcare innovations in our lifetimes.

Smart health insurance

Talk about how insurance will automatically get determined by smart contracts, giving certainty to patients, reducing manual effort for insurance companies and making sure records are imprinted on the blockchain

Health provider marketplace

Talk about how we're going to create a market of doctors who can provide health tips based on really customized information which represents personalized medicine and how it's amazing and how they're going to get ranked and patients get choose the best one like they see in Yelp

Clinical trials recruitment

talk about how drug companies and health authorities can select patients to recruit for clinical trials or to adjust their public messaging. Patient privacy is preserved and patients have to give express approval to allow them to see it. They will receive a token payment if their data is selected and they approve it

Durable health records

Talk about how patients can work with thier laboratories so they can automatically post their encrypted lab results to the Blockchain but no one will ever be able to read it without the patient actually giving approval. This makes record keeping trivial but empowers the patient so they're always controlling the flow of information

Critical secure access

Talk about how with the blockchian implementation of personalized medicine, if you showed up in the hospital catatonic the doctor could immediately retrieve all your medical history provigin you life saving treatment without delay, trying to identify you or waiting for your medical records

Smart health insurance

talk about how you could get automatically insurance approval for all the different procedures or treatments you qualify for immediately upon availability of your lab results, so no waiting around nervously for the insurance company

Personalized treatment

talk about how you could get customized, life saving treatment based on your genomic data. Talk about how everyone responds differently to different drugs and you'd get personalized treatment to minimize side effects, get accurate dosing so you get the best treatment the first time

Anonymous population data

talk about how public health authorities can make sweeping, timely recommendations based on more information being available on the blockchain. Public health authorities can also get alerted to localized, spreading symptoms so they could potentially prevent a pandemic!
